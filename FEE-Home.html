OUR STORY

Founded in 2018, From Every Example is an online media-platform that aims to educate people on different topics, share amazing stories about successful people, promote moral values and inner peace. All these ideas are embraced by Islam. Thus, we aim to create a moderate media-discourse from an Islamic point of view. As we believe that making this world a better place is a worship, and everyone's responsibility. We believe in the importance of knowledge, hard-smart working, morals and spirituality. Moreover, we strongly believe in the role coexistence and peace play in promoting healthy individuals, families and societies. Watch out videos and enjoy our content!
